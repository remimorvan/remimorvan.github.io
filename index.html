About me

Since September 2021, I am a Ph.D. student at LaBRI, Université de Bordeaux, under the supervision of Diego Figueira and Nathanaël Fijalkow, in the Formal Methods and Models department, in the RATIO, LX and Synth teams.

CV: in English / in French.
Contact: x@u-bordeaux.fr where x = remi.morvan (in French or in English).

I am interested in logic with an emphasis on database theory, automata, algebraic language theory and finite model theory.

Current responsibilities:

  • Member of LaBRI's scientific council.
  • Substitute member of the council of the STEM department of Univ. Bordeaux.
