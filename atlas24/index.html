About

One of the goal of the ATLAS workshop is to gather the automata community and pursue the collaborations started with ANR Frec and ANR Delta, on the themes related to automata theory and its connection with algebra and logic.

This workshop will be followed by the journées du GT DAAL, and is co-located with RaPS (Workshop on Pomsets and Related Structures).

It will take place in downtown Rennes, at EPITA (19—22 bd Saint-Conwoïon), from Tuesday 23 April in the morning to Thursday 25 at noon.

Organizers

  • Thomas Colcombet
  • Hugo Bazille
  • Uli Fahrenberg
